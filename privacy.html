Fly Space-A Privacy Policy

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for Fly Space-A. This privacy policy applies solely to information collected by Fly Space-A.

User Collection, Use, and Sharing

We may store name, email, and other pertinent user info granted permission by the user to authenticate users and to provide a personalized, high quality user experience.

Device Information

We collect metrics and anonymous crash information for debugging purposes. Crash information is only used for determining the cause(s) of a crash and used to help with fixing of crashes.
